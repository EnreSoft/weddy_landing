Policy Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

We have a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behavior, or activity that promotes, facilitates, or attempts to engage in CSAE is strictly prohibited on our platform.

We are committed to identifying, removing, and reporting any such content or behavior in compliance with legal requirements and platform guidelines. We work with relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC), to report violations.

Safety Measures

To help protect our users, we employ the following:

  • Automated and human content moderation systems.
  • Abuse reporting tools.
  • Collaboration with law enforcement and relevant organizations.

How to Report

If you encounter any content that may be related to CSAE, please report it immediately.
