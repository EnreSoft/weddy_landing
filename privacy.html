Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Weddy Online

Last Updated: 05.12.2025

Welcome to Weddy Online! This Privacy Policy explains how Weddy Online (“we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("Weddy Online”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account, including your name, email address, and password.
  • Update your profile with additional details such as your wedding event information, guest lists, and photos.
  • Contact us for support or inquiries.

1.2 Location Data

With your permission, we may collect your location data to:

  • Enhance event experiences by allowing location-based event features.
  • Display nearby wedding events for ease of navigation.
  • Improve personalized services based on your location.

You may choose to disable location access through your device settings.

1.3 Camera and External Storage Access

We request access to:

  • Camera: To allow you to capture and upload photos and videos directly to your wedding event.
  • External Storage: To select and upload images and videos from your device to share with event participants.

You can disable camera and storage access at any time via your device settings, but this may limit some features of the app.

1.4 Usage Data

We collect information on how you use Weddy Online, including:

  • Interactions with event pages, photos, stories, and guestbook entries.
  • Device information such as operating system, app version, and device model.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalize your experience within Weddy App.
  • Manage your account and provide customer service.
  • Facilitate photo and video uploads to your wedding event.
  • Improve app features and develop new services.
  • Send you notifications about event updates, changes, and invitations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information:

  • With event hosts to manage guest lists, RSVPs, and event updates.
  • With service providers that assist us with app operations, such as cloud storage.
  • If required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.

4. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously and implement security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your data from our servers.
  • Withdraw consent for location, camera, and storage access at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Weddy Online is not intended for children under the age of 12. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@weddyonline.com
